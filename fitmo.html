Launch Project

Fitness Motivator is an app that offers video fitness courses and an achievement tracking system that keeps you motivated and fit. Not only can you gain insights into health and fitness from this app, but you can also share your growth and achievements with online communities and be a part of the inspiration!

Problem

For many, exercise is boring and tedious. There is a need for constant motivation and guidance for workouts.
