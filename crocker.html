The Crocker Art Museum features the world’s foremost display of art and is renowned for its holdings of European master drawings and international ceramics. This project aims to rebrand the Crocker Art Museum and to market its featured exhibitions.

Design Guideline & Mood Board

To interpret the nature of fine art, the brand identity aims to be classic, elegant, yet simple enough to leave an impression on modern visitors.
